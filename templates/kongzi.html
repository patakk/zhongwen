第十二课 Lesson 12

第二单元 Unit 2

复习 (fùxí) Review

Negation and question forms:

  • 不吃 (bù chī) - don't eat
  • 不喝 (bù hē) - don't drink
  • 不难 (bù nán) - not difficult
  • 不忙 (bù máng) - not busy
  • 不想 (bù xiǎng) - don't want
  • 不喜欢 (bù xǐhuan) - don't like

Question forms:

  • 吃不吃？(chī bu chī) - Eat or not?
  • 喝不喝？(hē bu hē) - Drink or not?
  • 难不难？(nán bu nán) - Difficult or not?
  • 忙不忙？(máng bu máng) - Busy or not?
  • 想不想？(xiǎng bu xiǎng) - Want or not?
  • 喜不喜欢？(xǐ bu xǐhuan) - Like or not?

Conversation 1

A：你觉得汉语怎么样？(nǐ juéde hànyǔ zěnmeyàng) - What do you think of Chinese?

B：汉语很有意思。(hànyǔ hěn yǒuyìsi) - Chinese is interesting.

A：难不难？(nán bu nán) - Is it difficult?

B：不太难。(bù tài nán) - Not too difficult.

Conversation 3

A：这个叫什么？(zhège jiào shénme) - What do you call this (in Chinese)?

B：苹果。(píngguǒ) - Apple.

A：那个叫什么？(nàge jiào shénme) - What do you call that (in Chinese)?

B：香蕉。你要不要。(xiāngjiāo. nǐ yào bu yào) - Banana. Do you want (to buy)?

A：我不要。(wǒ bú yào) - No.

Conversation 4

A：今天几号？(jīntiān jǐ hào) - What's the date today?

B：今天十二月二十五号。(jīntiān shí'èr yuè èrshíwǔ hào) - Today is December 25th.

A：今天天气不错。(jīntiān tiānqì bú cuò) - It's a fine day.

B：是啊，不冷不热，很舒服。(shì a, bù lěng bù rè, hěn shūfu) - Yes. It's neither cold nor hot. It's very comfortable.

Grammar Points

Question Patterns

1. ...吗？(...ma?)

  • 你是老师吗？(nǐ shì lǎoshī ma) - Are you a teacher?
  • 是。/我是老师。(shì. / wǒ shì lǎoshī) - Yes. / I am a teacher.
  • 不。/我不是老师。(bù. / wǒ bú shì lǎoshī) - No. / I am not a teacher.

2. 是不是 (shì bú shì) / A不A

  • 你是不是克罗地亚人？(nǐ shì bú shì kèluódìyà rén) - Are you Croatian?
  • 你吃不吃汉堡？(nǐ chī bu chī hànbǎo) - Do you eat hamburgers?

3. AB phrase: A不AB

  • 你喜不喜欢吃汉堡？(nǐ xǐ bu xǐhuan chī hànbǎo) - Do you like eating hamburgers?

也 (yě) - also

Examples:

  • 我也是老师。(wǒ yě shì lǎoshī) - I am also a teacher.
  • 我也喝可乐。(wǒ yě hē kělè) - I also drink cola.

都 (dōu) - both/all

Example:

  • 我们都是克罗地亚人。(wǒmen dōu shì kèluódìyà rén) - We are all Croatian.

只 (zhǐ) - only

Example:

  • 我只吃汉堡。(wǒ zhǐ chī hànbǎo) - I only eat hamburgers.

还是 (háishì) - or (in questions)

Example:

  • 你喜欢咖啡还是可乐？(nǐ xǐhuan kāfēi háishì kělè) - Do you like coffee or cola?

Open Questions

什么 (shénme) - what

哪 (nǎ) - which

Examples:

  • 你叫什么名字？(nǐ jiào shénme míngzi) - What's your name?
  • 你是哪国人？(nǐ shì nǎ guó rén) - Which country are you from?

New Lesson

Entertaining guests in Chinese culture:

  • 请坐 (qǐng zuò) - Please sit
  • 你的公司怎么样 (nǐ de gōngsī zěnmeyàng) - How is your company?
  • 这是我的女朋友 (zhè shì wǒ de nǚpéngyou) - This is my girlfriend
  • 他的女朋友很漂亮 (tā de nǚpéngyou hěn piàoliang) - His girlfriend is very beautiful
  • 你喜欢喝中国茶吗 (nǐ xǐhuan hē zhōngguó chá ma) - Do you like drinking Chinese tea?
  • 我在英国东方学院中文系学习 (wǒ zài yīngguó dōngfāng xuéyuàn zhōngwén xì xuéxí) - I study Chinese at the Oriental Institute in England

  • 那些 (nàxiē) - those

#3 请 (qǐng) - please

Used with actions like:

  • 坐 (zuò) - sit
  • 看 (kàn) - look
  • 吃 (chī) - eat
  • 喝茶 (hē chá) - drink tea
  • 进 (jìn) - enter

请 also means "to treat":

  • 我请你喝咖啡 (wǒ qǐng nǐ hē kāfēi) - I'll treat you to coffee
  • 我请你吃饭 (wǒ qǐng nǐ chīfàn) - I'll treat you to a meal

#4 怎么样 (zěnmeyàng) - how

Used to ask about state or condition:

  • 你怎么样？(nǐ zěnmeyàng) - How are you?

  • 我还行 (wǒ hái xíng) - I'm okay
  • 你的学习怎么样？(nǐ de xuéxí zěnmeyàng) - How's your studying?
  • 我的学习很好 (wǒ de xuéxí hěn hǎo) - My studying is going well
  • 一般般 (yībānbān) - So-so

  • 那些是我的同学（们）。(nàxiē shì wǒ de tóngxué (men)) - Those are my classmates.
#3 请 (qǐng) - please

Used with actions like:

  • 坐 (zuò) - sit
  • 看 (kàn) - look
  • 吃 (chī) - eat
  • 喝茶 (hē chá) - drink tea
  • 进 (jìn) - enter

请 also means "to treat":

  • 我请你喝咖啡。(wǒ qǐng nǐ hē kāfēi) - I'll treat you to coffee.
  • 我请你吃饭。(wǒ qǐng nǐ chīfàn) - I'll treat you to a meal.
#4 怎么样 (zěnmeyàng) - how

Used to ask about state or condition:

  • 你怎么样？(nǐ zěnmeyàng) - How are you?
  • 我很好。(wǒ hěn hǎo) - I'm very good.
  • 我还行。(wǒ hái xíng) - I'm okay.
  • 你的学习怎么样？(nǐ de xuéxí zěnmeyàng) - How's your studying?
  • 我的学习很好。(wǒ de xuéxí hěn hǎo) - My studying is going well.
  • 一般般。(yībānbān) - So-so.

语法课 (yǔfǎ kè) Grammar Lesson

Grammar 1: Possessive Pronouns

你的 (nǐde) - Your 你们的 (nǐmende) - Your (plural)
我的 (wǒde) - My 我们的 (wǒmende) - Our
他的 (tāde) - His 他们的 (tāmende) - Their (masculine)
她的 (tāde) - Her 她们的 (tāmende) - Their (feminine)
它的 (tāde) - Its 它们的 (tāmende) - Their (neutral)

Grammar 2: 在 (zài) - at/in

Location words:

  • 公司 (gōngsī) - company
  • 医院 (yīyuàn) - hospital
  • 大学 (dàxué) - university
  • 饭店 (fàndiàn) - restaurant
  • 咖啡馆 (kāfēiguǎn) - café
  • 家 (jiā) - home

Example questions and answers:

  • 你在哪儿工作？(nǐ zài nǎr gōngzuò) - Where do you work?
  • 我在医院工作。(wǒ zài yīyuàn gōngzuò) - I work at the hospital.
  • 你在哪儿喝咖啡？(nǐ zài nǎr hē kāfēi) - Where do you drink coffee?
  • 我在咖啡馆喝咖啡。(wǒ zài kāfēiguǎn hē kāfēi) - I drink coffee at the café.
  • 你在哪儿吃汉堡？(nǐ zài nǎr chī hànbǎo) - Where do you eat hamburgers?
  • 我在Burger King（汉堡王）吃汉堡。(wǒ zài Burger King (hànbǎo wáng) chī hànbǎo) - I eat hamburgers at Burger King.
  • 你在哪儿看电视？(nǐ zài nǎr kàn diànshì) - Where do you watch TV?
  • 我在家看电视。(wǒ zài jiā kàn diànshì) - I watch TV at home.

Grammar 3: Demonstrative Pronouns

  • 这 (zhè) - this
  • 这个 (zhège) - this (with quantifier)
  • 这些 (zhèxiē) - these
  • 那 (nà) - that
  • 那个 (nàge) - that (with quantifier)
  • 那些 (nàxiē) - those

Examples:

  • 这是我的男朋友。(zhè shì wǒ de nánpéngyou) - This is my boyfriend.
  • 那是他的女朋友。(nà shì tā de nǚpéngyou) - That is his girlfriend.
  • 这些是我的老师（们）。(zhèxiē shì wǒ de lǎoshī (men)) - These are my teachers.
  • 那些是我的同学（们）。(nàxiē shì wǒ de tóngxué (men)) - Those are my classmates.

Grammar 4: Describing Quality

Structure: Subject + 非常 (fēicháng) + Adjective

Example: 这个电影非常好。(zhège diànyǐng fēicháng hǎo) - This movie is very good.

How to ask about quality: Subject + 怎么样？(zěnmeyàng)

Other examples:

  • 我的答案对，你的答案不对。(wǒ de dá'àn duì, nǐ de dá'àn bú duì) - My answer is correct, your answer is incorrect.
  • 我的答案对，你的答案错。(wǒ de dá'àn duì, nǐ de dá'àn cuò) - My answer is right, your answer is wrong.
