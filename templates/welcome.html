Welcome {{ session['username'] }}!

here's a quick guide to get you started:

  • Hanzi Grid - preview characters and their descriptions
    • Clicking on the title toggles between grid and list view.
    • If you start typing, what you type will be searched, and filtered results will be shown
      • Both pinyin and english queries are supported.
      • Press Space to clear the filter.
  • Search - find words (english/hanzi/pinyin supported)
    • The following pinyin formats are supported:
      • tone numbers (e.g. 'ni3 hao3', or ni3 ha3o)
      • tone marks (e.g. 'nǐ hǎo')
      • no tones (e.g. 'ni hao')
  • Flashcards - test and learn characters using spaced repetition
    • Press Space to reveal the answer.
    • Hold Space to mark as correct.
    • Hold X to mark as incorrect.
    • Or, click and hold right side of the flashcard for correct, left side for incorrect.
    • Tap to reveal the answer.
    • Swipe right to mark as correct.
    • Swipe left to mark as incorrect.
  • Example Lists - learn grammar and characters in context
